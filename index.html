About Me

I am an associate professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Auburn University.

My research currently focuses on the identification and quantification of uncertainty in physical systems modeled by (partial) differential equations.

I earned my PhD from Virginia Tech, working under the supervision of Dr. Jeff Borggaard. My PhD dissertation "#############################" can be downloaded here.

I completed my postdoctoral training at FSU, working under the supervision of Dr. Maz Gunzburger.
